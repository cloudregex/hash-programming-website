Our Story

From a Small Idea to a Big Dream

Hash Programing Coding Classes was founded in 2025 by a group of passionate developers who believed that quality coding education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or financial situation.

What started as weekend workshops in a small co-working space has now grown into one of India's most trusted coding academies. Our journey has been fueled by one simple belief: everyone deserves the chance to learn to code.

Today, we've helped over 25+ students transform their careers, with graduates now working at top companies like Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and countless startups.
